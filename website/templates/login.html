Scouter On Glass

Scouter On Glass finds the power levels of people in pictures you take with your Google Glass™, similar to how the Scouter works in Dragon Ball Z.

Simply take a picture of the target, share it with the Scouter icon, and their power level will be reported in a few seconds. Be prepared to yell "IT'S OVER 9000!!!!".

To install it, simply sign in below and the Scouter icon will be added to your Glass Share menu for any pictures you take.
